Caprese Salad

Zutaten

2 Portionen

3 mediumtomatoes
2 packsmozzarella
0,5 cupfresh basil
etwasflaky salt
etwasblack pepper
2 tbspextra virgin olive oil
2 tbspbalsamic glaze

Zubereitung

3 medium tomatoes, 2 packs mozzarella

Place the tomatoes and mozzarella on a platter. Arrange tomatoes and mozzarella on a platter in an alternating pattern.

0,5 cup fresh basil

Top with the fresh basil. Scatter the basil leaves over the tomatoes and mozzarella.

etwas flaky salt, etwas black pepper

Season with flaky salt and black pepper. Sprinkle with a generous pinch of flaky salt and several grinds of black pepper, to taste.

2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, 2 tbsp balsamic glaze

Drizzle with the extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze. Drizzle the olive oil and balsamic glaze over the tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. Serve immediately.
