Sicilian-style Scottadito Lamb Chops

Zutaten

4 Portionen

8 largelamb chops
2 clovesgarlic
1 tspfennel seeds
2 springsfresh oregano
1 splashextra virgin olive oil
1 pinchsalt
1 pinchground cinnamon
etwaslemon
etwassalt
etwasblack pepper

Zubereitung

Light the barbecue and let it get nice and hot. Make sure the bars of the barbecue are clean and free from any burnt on residue.

8 large lamb chops

Take the lamb chops out of the firdge and place on a plate to come up to to room temperatur. Meanwhile...

2 cloves garlic, 1 tsp fennel seeds

Peel the garlic. Bash the fennel seeds in a pestle and mortar until fine.

2 springs fresh oregano, 1 splash extra virgin olive oil, 1 pinch salt

Pick and add the leaves from fresh oregano, the garlic, extra virgin olive oil and salt and bash again.

1 pinch ground cinnamon, etwas lemon

Stir in the ground cinnamon, zest the lemon over the top and mix again. Tip into a roasting tray and spread out.

Lightly splash the fat in several places along the chops.

etwas salt, etwas black pepper

Place the chops in one layer on a board, season well with salt and black pepper then bash and flatten slightly with the base of a small saucepan.

Transfer the chops to the roasting tray, then turn over in the marinade to coat, then leave to marinate for around 30 minutes.

When the bars of the grill are nice and hot, drop the chops onto the hot side and sear on botth side for 2 minutes, or until browned all over (move the chops away from any flamse that appear).

Cut the lemon in half with a clean knife, and squeeze over the chops for the last minute of cooking, then transver to a plate to rest before serving.
